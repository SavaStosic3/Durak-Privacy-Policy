# Durak Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

This privacy policy covers Durak, a free offline card game for iOS, Android, and
web.

Developer: Sava Stosic

## Summary

Durak does not collect personal data from the app.

The app does not require an account, does not ask for sign-in, does not include
ads, does not include analytics SDKs, and does not run online multiplayer
services.

Durak offers optional in-app purchases for cosmetic theme packs only. These are
processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play; Durak does not collect or
receive your payment details and stores only a local record of which cosmetic
packs you own.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Durak stores some game data locally on your device so the app can work:

- gameplay settings;
- saved or resumable games;
- local stats;
- which cosmetic theme packs you own (earned or purchased);
- local replay or bug-report export data generated by the app.

This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to Durak servers because
Durak does not have a backend service.

You can remove this local data by deleting the app or clearing the app's local
storage using your device or browser controls.

## Optional Bug Reports

Durak can generate local bug-report export data to help diagnose gameplay
issues. This export is created on your device. It leaves the app only if you
choose to copy or share it outside the app.

If you voluntarily send a bug report or support message, we may receive the
information you choose to send and use it only to investigate the issue and
respond.

## In-App Purchases

Durak offers optional one-time purchases for cosmetic theme packs. They are
purely cosmetic and never change game rules, bot strength, stats, skill scoring,
achievements, or offline play. Every paid pack can also be earned for free
through achievements.

Purchases are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Those
stores process your payment and any associated data under their own privacy
policies; Durak does not receive your payment card or billing details. After a
purchase, Durak stores only a local record that you own the pack and asks the
store to confirm ownership when you use "Restore Purchases".

## Third Parties

Durak does not include third-party advertising, analytics, authentication, or
social SDKs. Payment for optional cosmetic purchases is processed by the Apple
App Store or Google Play, not by Durak.

The app is distributed through Apple, Google, and web hosting platforms. Those
platforms may process data under their own privacy policies when you use their
stores or services.

## Children

Durak does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes

If a future version adds accounts, online play, analytics, ads, or any other
data collection, this policy and the store privacy declarations must be updated
before release.
